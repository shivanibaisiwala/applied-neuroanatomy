Applied Neuroanatomy

Applied Neuroanatomy is an interactive platform for surgical education, organized around the eight cranial corridors. Anatomy, radiology, key concepts, and operative pearls — structured for independent review and case-based teaching.

Anatomy images are adapted from the Rhoton Cranial Anatomy and Surgical Approaches collection, used for educational purposes with attribution to Albert L. Rhoton Jr. and the Rhoton Collection at neurosurgicalatlas.com.

This site is for educational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Clinical decisions should be based on individual patient assessment, current evidence, and attending physician guidance.
